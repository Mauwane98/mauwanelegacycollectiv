In Honour

The Hands That Taught Us

“IIf I had been born in your generation, I would have gone much further by now. With the resources and opportunities available today, my business would have reached new heights.” - Rapudi Moses Mauwane

Rapudi Moses Mauwane was more than a skilled leatherworker—he was a visionary ahead of his time. His dedication, resourcefulness, and pride in handmade excellence laid the groundwork for what Mauwane Legacy Collective stands for today. We honour his influence not only through our craft, but by creating new paths from the ones he carved with his own hands.
