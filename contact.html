Frequently Asked Questions

We respond to all inquiries within 2 business days. For urgent matters, please call our customer service line during business hours.

Yes, we ship worldwide through our Mossé Luxe brand. LSG Creations currently ships within South Africa only. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout.

Our minimum order quantity varies by product line. For Mossé Luxe leather goods, the MOQ is 10 pieces per style. For LSG Creations educational products, the MOQ is 20 units per item.
